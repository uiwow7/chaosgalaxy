CG Database Search Help

To search by things other than name, use the denoted terms, followed by a colon and what you want to search by (to include multiple words, use quotation marks). For example, to search by stars you could do stars:8 and to search for cards that draw a card you could do text:"draw a card".

a, text, ability: Search by card ability.

s, star, stars: Search by card star cost.

h, hea, health: Search by card health.

pow, power: Search by card power.

p, planet: Search by card planet.

t, type, cardtype: Search by card planet.

b, ban, banned, banlist: Search by card banlist status.
